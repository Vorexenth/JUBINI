About NOHS

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and strong moral values in our students, preparing them to become responsible and productive citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, cultural enrichment, and holistic student development in the region.

Our History

Founded in [Year], Negros Occidental High School has been a cornerstone of education in Bacolod City for generations. Our school was established with the vision of providing accessible, quality education to the youth of Negros Occidental.

Throughout the decades, NOHS has evolved from a modest institution into one of the premier secondary schools in the region. We have continuously adapted our curriculum and facilities to meet the changing needs of our students and society.

Our rich heritage is reflected in the thousands of successful alumni who have made significant contributions to various fields, from medicine and law to arts and public service. The NOHS spirit of excellence, integrity, and service continues to inspire each new generation of students.

Today, NOHS stands as a testament to the power of dedicated educators, supportive community, and motivated students working together toward a common goal of educational excellence.
