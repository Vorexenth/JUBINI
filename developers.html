About This Project

This project is dedicated to creating the website of Negros Occidental High School, a collaborative effort that brings together different roles and talents. It is the result of teamwork between researchers, designers, web developers, leaders, and presenters, each contributing their expertise to build a platform that represents the school’s identity and serves its community. The goal is to provide a reliable, informative, and user-friendly website that showcases the school’s values, achievements, and resources, while also fostering communication and accessibility for students, teachers, and visitors.
